Flappy Pokemon!

Welcome to Flappy Pokemon.

Charizard has lost Ash in the Cerulean Cave, and he seems to have woken up a flock of Articunos! Help Charizard find Ash and the others safely without running into any of the Articuno Flock. But be careful, crashing into an Articuno will push you back to the start!

Here will be the game

How to play:

  • Press SPACE to flap your wings.
  • Avoid the Articunos.
  • If you crash, just try again!
